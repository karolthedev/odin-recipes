Pasta Salad

Description:

Pasta Salad is a vibrant, refreshing dish perfect for gatherings or a light meal. Packed with colorful vegetables, a zesty dressing, and savory seasoning, it's as delicious as it is easy to prepare.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound tri-colored spiral pasta
  • 1 bottle Italian-style dressing (or homemade)
  • 1 packet salad seasoning mix
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 1 yellow bell pepper
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 can black olives

Steps:

Follow these simple steps to make a delicious Pasta Salad:

  1. Cook and drain the pasta.
  2. Whisk the dressing with the seasoning mix.
  3. Combine the pasta with the vegetables.
  4. Toss everything in the seasoned dressing.
